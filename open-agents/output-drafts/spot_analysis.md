## Company Overview

Spotify Technology S.A. is the world's largest audio streaming platform, offering on-demand access to 100M+ tracks, 6M podcast titles, and 400K+ audiobooks across 184 markets. The company operates a freemium model with 713M monthly active users (281M premium subscribers).

**CEO:** Daniel Ek | **Founded:** 2006 | **IPO:** April 2018 (NYSE) | **Employees:** ~9,000

## Competitive Position

**Moat Rating:** Wide

### U.S. Music Streaming Market Share
- **Spotify:** 37% (713M MAUs, 281M Premium)
- **Apple Music:** 30.7% (~110M users)
- **Amazon Music:** 23.8% (~90M users)
- **YouTube Music:** 9.7% (~92M users)

### Competitive Advantages
1. **Scale & Network Effects** — Largest user base enables superior AI recommendations
2. **Data & Personalization** — Billions of data points feed curation (Discover Weekly, AI DJ)
3. **Platform Diversification** — Music + Podcasts + Audiobooks + Video
4. **Freemium Funnel** — Free tier acquires users at scale; Premium converts
5. **Global Reach** — 184 markets, far exceeding rivals

## Risks and Concerns

1. **Valuation Premium:** Trailing P/E ~61x and EV/EBITDA ~51x remain elevated
2. **Royalty Cost Pressure:** $11B paid in 2025 royalties; labels negotiating higher rates
3. **Big Tech Competition:** Apple, Amazon, Google can subsidize streaming as loss leaders
4. **Regulatory Risk:** EU DMA "gatekeeper" classification push could increase burden
5. **Litigation:** Class action lawsuits, indie label pushback on 1,000-stream threshold
6. **AI Content Fraud:** AI-generated content flooding platform to game royalties
