🎵 Company Overview

Spotify Technology S.A. is the world's largest audio streaming subscription service, headquartered in Stockholm, Sweden. Founded in 2006 by Daniel Ek and Martin Lorentzon, the platform offers on-demand access to over 100 million tracks, 6 million podcast titles, and 400,000+ audiobooks across 184 markets worldwide.


The company operates a freemium model: a free ad-supported tier drives user acquisition, while the Premium tier ($11.99/month individual) generates the majority of revenue. Spotify has been aggressively diversifying beyond music into podcasts, audiobooks, and video content, pursuing what CEO Daniel Ek calls the "ubiquity strategy" — becoming the default audio platform across every device and context.
